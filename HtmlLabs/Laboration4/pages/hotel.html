People of Baarah

Here you will get all the content about our People and kulture

Is an inhabited island in Haa Alif Atoll in the northern Maldives. It is an island-level administrative constiturency governed by the Baarah Island Council Historically, Baarah was the island where the vessel Kalhuohfummiwas built in the latter half of the 16th century, The ship played a key role in Sultan Muhammad Thakurafaanu's war against the portuguese invation of the country. The sails of the ship were made on the island of Moroshi futher south. The island is shaped like the letter 'C'. A larg part of the shores are covered by a mangoves predominantly occupied by Rhizophora mucronata and Hibiscus tillaceus. The soil around these areas is rich in hydrogen sulphide and often avoided by the islanders. The eccosystem is under thret due to logging and garbage dumping and poor management.
